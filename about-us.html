ABOUT US

What is the RGU Music Society?

Welcome to the RGU Music Society!

While only recently formed, we at RGU Music are dedicated to help those who are interested in music explore all posibilities. We are building with the ability to help you organise venues for your events, for finding like minded musicians to form that group you've always been eager to make, and one to help those who are just getting started exploring this craft.

At RGU Music we understand that music is a social experience, but also how difficult it is to connect with other musicians coming off the back of Covid-19. To remedy this, we have many new upcoming events to introduce you to other excited ambitious musicians who are also looking for others so that they can finally start to make the music they've been waiting years to make.

However, maybe you feel you're not quite ready to get out there and play your music yet. This is absolute fine, and we have many members who are more than happy to help you hone your craft, equally, there are many members who find it helpful to learn along with someone else so please feel free to join us if you are just beginning your journey with music!

How can we help you to organise?

At the RGU Music Society our most important goal is to make communication and collaboration as easy as possible. Using the Contact Us page, you can get in touch if you would like to join us in some of our existing events or maybe you have an idea you’d love to bring to our society.

If you’d like to know more, please feel free to visit the Contact Us page!
