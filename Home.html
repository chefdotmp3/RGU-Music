What is the RGU Music Society?

Welcome to the RGU Music Society!

Welcome to the RGU Music Society! This society is a way for people interested in music to organise with eachother to form events, groups, or even just talk to like minded people. Maybe you’re looking for people to study with, or maybe you have an interest but haven’t been able to start - regardless this is the place for you.

How can you join?

We at the RGU Music Society are always looking for new members with fresh ideas to join our inclusive community and make it the best it can possibly be.

If you are interested in joining our group or an existing member looking to organise, you should visit the Contact Us page where you can find information on how best to get in touch with us.

We are eager to hear from you, and more than happy to answer any questions or ideas for potential events you have for us, so please, get in touch with us!
